# AI Cliché Blocklist

Every phrase below is banned. These are the fingerprints of AI-generated text. Using any of them breaks the "100% human-written" guarantee.

Check this list before writing AND after writing. If any match appears in the draft, rewrite the sentence — don't just swap the word.

---

## English Phrases (banned even in PT-PT content — they leak as calques)

### Verbs and verb phrases
- delve / delve into
- dive deep / dive into
- leverage (as a verb — "to leverage X")
- utilize (say "use")
- facilitate (say what actually happens)
- foster
- navigate (metaphorical — "navigate challenges", "navigate the landscape")
- embark / embark on a journey
- unpack ("let's unpack this")
- underscore (as "to emphasize")
- streamline
- revolutionize
- harness ("harness the power of")
- empower (overused)
- unlock ("unlock your potential", "unlock new possibilities")

### Adjectives and buzzwords
- seamless / seamlessly
- cutting-edge
- robust
- holistic
- comprehensive (say what it actually covers)
- multifaceted
- paramount
- pivotal (use "key", "critical", or be specific)
- groundbreaking
- innovative (show, don't label)
- game-changing / game-changer
- transformative
- impactful
- scalable (in non-technical contexts)
- synergy / synergistic
- dynamic

### Filler openers and transitions
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "In the ever-evolving landscape of..."
- "In the realm of..."
- "It is worth noting that..."
- "It is important to note that..."
- "It goes without saying that..."
- "As we know..." / "As you know..."
- "Needless to say..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- "Moving forward..." / "Going forward..."
- "That being said..."
- "With that in mind..."
- "All things considered..."
- "In light of this..."

### Closings (never end with these)
- "In conclusion..."
- "To summarize..."
- "To wrap up..."
- "In summary..."
- "As we have seen..."
- "Ultimately, it is clear that..."

### Miscellaneous
- "paradigm shift"
- "best practices" (be specific instead — what practices, exactly?)
- "thought leader" / "thought leadership"
- "value proposition" (in non-marketing contexts)
- "pain points" (in non-sales contexts)
- "touch base"
- "circle back"
- "take this to the next level"
- "low-hanging fruit"
- "move the needle"
- "deep dive" (as a noun — "let's do a deep dive")

## Portuguese Calques and Banned Phrases

### Calques from English AI patterns
- "mergulhar em" / "mergulhar fundo" (calque of "dive deep")
- "alavancar" used as "leverage" in non-financial contexts
- "navegar" used metaphorically ("navegar os desafios", "navegar o mercado")
- "desvendar" used as "unpack"
- "capacitar" / "empoderar" used as empowerment cliché
- "aproveitar ao máximo" as stock phrase opener

### Cliché openers (PT-PT)
- "No mundo cada vez mais rápido..."
- "No panorama atual..."
- "No mundo em constante evolução..."
- "É importante salientar que..."
- "É de notar que..."
- "Como sabemos..."
- "Dito isto..."
- "Tendo isto em conta..."
- "Num contexto de..."

### Cliché closings (PT-PT)
- "Em conclusão..."
- "Em resumo..."
- "Para concluir..."
- "Em suma..."
- "Como podemos ver..."
- "No final do dia..." (direct calque)

### Buzzwords in Portuguese
- "sinergia" (unless genuinely technical)
- "holístico" (overused)
- "robusto" (in non-engineering contexts)
- "inovador" (show it, don't label it)
- "transformador" / "transformativo"
- "disruptivo" (unless discussing actual disruption theory)
- "paradigma" (unless Kuhn is actually relevant)

---

## Rule

If a sentence needs one of these phrases to work, the sentence is wrong. Rewrite the idea from scratch.
